Research

Research Interests

My research agenda connects civil engineering practice with digital construction methods, focusing on problems that matter to site management, safety, coordination, and engineering education.

BIM-Based Construction Management

I am interested in how BIM can support design coordination, schedule visualization, clash reduction, and decision-making processes in complex construction projects.

Digital Twin in Construction

I aim to explore digital twin frameworks that integrate project progress, safety observations, resource allocation, and quality data for real-time site management.

Safety Training Systems

I study how digital teaching systems, simulation, and scenario-based training can improve construction safety awareness and professional competence among engineering students and practitioners.

Engineering Project Optimization

I am interested in optimization methods for schedule control, resource planning, risk management, and multi-stakeholder coordination in large engineering projects.
